# SirenMan


# About SirenMan

SirenMan is a iOS app that let's you play police sirens, use your phone as a microphone and play audio clips from cops in GTA V. The app is built using Swift in Xcode. **This is app is for educational purposes only and should not be used in any illegal manner.**

# Features
- Police Mic: Using your phone, you can use your car speakers / aux to amplify your voice
- Variety of Sirens + Horn: You have 3 realistic sirens to choose from and a horn sound
- Police Radio Chatter: You have the option to play police radio chatter on loop
- "Move it Folks" Sound: Used the "Move it Folks" from GTA V.
